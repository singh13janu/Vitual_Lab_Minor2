Experiment : Thermodynamic Power Plant

Aim

The primary aim of thermodynamics is to provide a framework for describing and predicting the behavior of physical systems in terms of energy transfer and transformation. This includes understanding how energy flows between different components of a system and its surroundings, as well as predicting the efficiency and feasibility of various processes.

Theory

The theory of thermodynamics is a fundamental framework that governs the behavior of energy and its interactions within physical systems. It encompasses several key principles and laws that are essential for understanding and predicting the behavior of various natural and engineered systems.
